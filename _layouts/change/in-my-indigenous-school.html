  • Indigenous Leadership Structures

    A growing number of schools are managed by Indigenous communities and nations themselves.

    Here are just a few examples:

    • British Columbia has a First Nations Education Steering Committee that has board-like decisionmaking power for almost all the First Nations schools in British Columbia: fnesc.ca
    • The Innu in Labrador have their own school board: innueducation.ca
    • The James Bay Cree in Quebec also have their own school board: cscree.qc.ca/en/our-organization
